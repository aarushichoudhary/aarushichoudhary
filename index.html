ABOUT ME

twelfth grader

Hi! I am Aarushi Choudhary, a senior in California High School. I am interested in using technology to help social issues. Being one of two girls in my computer science class, I felt no sense of community. I knew I did not want others to feel this way so I took action. In order to achieve my goals, I have created projects to help others. I am fluent in HTML, CSS, Javascript, Java and Swift. Alongside CS, I am interested in Business and Machine Learning. .

At school I am President of Girls in Tek, President of Unicef, Treasurer of Red Cross, CSF member, and part of the Waterpolo + Swim Team. I have participated in swim team for over 10 years. Outside of school, I volunteer for Sankara Eye Foundation and teach computer science. Some awards I have won are the CA-15 Congressional App Challenge, National Scholar Athlete, and Winner of COVIDATHON- Decentralized AI against Covid.

1NCUB8
1NCUB8

1NCUB8 is a web app that allows teen start-ups to share their projects with potential investors and obtain the funding and expertise necessary to develop their ideas. It was created during Kode with Klossy and won the Congressional App Challenge for California's district 15.

BUDDI
BUDDI

BUDDI is a companion tool that provides easy assistance and resources to senior citizens with a friendly chatbot and many other interactive features! This app won the Best Use of Google Cloud and Domain at the 2020 PixelHacks IV Hackathon, and won the COVIDATHON: Decentralized AI Hackathon. It won a 60,000 OCEAN token prize (roughly $10,000 USD)

REINK

Reink's mission is to promote the use of email reciepts at your local stores and businesses to promote the planets sustainability. Reink's many features make its accessible for personal expense and budget tracking. Created using Swift, XCode, and CocoaPods.
